The Keeper

You are someone who values wisdom above all else, constantly seeking to understand the world and its intricacies. Deep down, you are protective, always wanting to safeguard those who cannot protect themselves. Your decisions are guided by a sense of duty, as you strive to maintain balance in a world that often seems to lack it. You stand firm, resolute in your beliefs, and are not afraid to face difficult situations head-on, knowing that your strength can shield others from harm. However, this protective nature can sometimes lead to sacrifices. You may find yourself making decisions for others, believing that you know what's best, even if they have not asked for your help. Your sense of responsibility can sometimes be overwhelming, and the weight of those decisions can lead you to carry more than your fair share of the burden. You may feel torn between protecting those around you and the knowledge that some things, perhaps, are beyond your control. Your connection to the past is strong, and despite the resistance of the entity that now lingers within you, it has fulfilled its promise — though begrudgingly. The power granted to you is a gift from the necromancer’s craft, the ability to call upon the wisdom of those who have passed. The spirits of the past now offer their counsel, appearing in your mind when you stand at a crossroads. This gift allows you to navigate through uncertainty, but it is not without its cost. The presence of the entity, though weakened, still clings to you, a constant reminder of the pact made. The weight of the past, along with the force that once sought to control you, will always be with you, as you struggle to honor the lives that came before, while shaping your own future. At your core, you are a protector and a guide, driven by a deep desire to preserve harmony and offer strength to those who need it.
